# ShapeShifter:
This role is to transform or impersonate or shift another player, And pretends to kill someone in front of everyone, the role was released in **V2021.11.1** 

- If you shift in a player you will be having:
  - [x] Player Skin
  - [x] Player Name (Above your head)
  - [x] Player Color Name (if colorblind text is on)
  - [x] Player Pet

- The Things that you will not be having is:
  - [] Player Tasks
